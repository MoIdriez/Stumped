Milestones

  • OBSEA: Dragon's Den

    February 2017

    After conducting preliminary research, Stumped! competed in a 'Dragon's Den' type competition chaired by Oxford Brookes Social Enterpreneursip Awards (OBSEA) with the view of receiving funding to further research our idea. The panel are from various backgrounds including: business, technology, healthcare and funding was offered courtasy of Santander Bank.

  • Global Challenge Nomination

    21st March 2017

    "Try it" Award
    After a successful pitch at the Dragon's Den, Stumped! were nominated to represent Oxford Brookes University in Global Challenge organised by Oxford University. Additionally, funding was awarded to conduct further investigations into our project idea.

  • University Newsletter

    26th April 2017

    Oxford Brookes University shared our success in representing the univeristy in a Global Challenge hosted by Oxford University.

  • Global Challenge Finalists

    1st May 2017

    After the nomination by Oxford Brookes University, Stumped! were shortlisted from 200 applications from different universities around the world to the top 12 teams from America To Asia.

  • Founders Day

    24th May 2017

    "Do it" Award
    Stumped! received further funding to implement a product based on the work done utilising 'Try It' award. Mentoring from OBSEA as well as Grant Thornton is ongoing.

  • Student Leadership

    June-September 2017

    An exciting opportunity for selected students across healthcare disciplines with particular leadership potential to further develop their skills through a four month long programme providing intensive training in the essential knowledge and skills for future healthcare leaders.

  • Presentation at OBSEA Launch

    4th October 2017

    Stumped! were invited to present at the 2017 launch of the Oxford Brookes Social Entrepeneureship Awards to share the potential all students have when going the extra mile.

  • Diabetic Foot Masterclass

    6th December 2017

    Stumped! were invited to the Conferece where the project was included in the section "Inovation from the nations / England" and promted and shared with experts in the area.
